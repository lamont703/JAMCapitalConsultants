Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

How long does credit repair take?
Credit repair is not an overnight process. The timeline varies based on your specific situation, but most clients see initial results within 30-45 days after disputes are filed. Significant improvements typically occur over 3-6 months of consistent effort. Our Full-Service and Guided plans can help expedite the process with expert strategies.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, all our paid plans can be canceled at any time with no cancellation fees. You'll continue to have access to your plan features until the end of your current billing cycle. The Free plan never expires and requires no payment information.
What's the difference between the plans?
The DIY plan provides you with all the tools and resources to repair your credit yourself. The Guided plan adds expert support and strategy sessions to help you along the way. The Full-Service plan is our white-glove service where we handle everything for you - from writing and sending dispute letters to following up with creditors and credit bureaus.
Is there a guarantee for credit score improvement?
While we cannot legally guarantee specific point increases (no credit repair company can), our methods have helped thousands of clients improve their credit scores. Results vary based on your unique credit situation, the accuracy of items on your report, and your financial behavior during the repair process.
What is the JAM Dispute Bot?
The JAM Dispute Bot is our AI-powered assistant that helps analyze your credit reports, identify disputable items, and generate customized dispute letters based on credit laws. It's available in all paid plans and provides 24/7 guidance for your credit repair journey.
