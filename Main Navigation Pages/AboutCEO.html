About the CEO

Meet Carnelius Johnson, the visionary leader behind JAM Capital Consultants LLC and your trusted partner in financial transformation.

Our Founder's Story

At the heart of JAM Capital Consultants LLC is a simple belief: Everyone deserves a fair chance to rebuild their credit and regain control over their financial future. Our Founder and CEO, Carnelius Johnson, knows this firsthand. After facing his own challenges with credit, Carnelius made it his mission to turn his experience into a way to help others rise above the same obstacles.

Carnelius is a dedicated credit professional with years of hands-on experience guiding individuals and business owners through the complex world of credit repair and education. His approach is direct, transparent, and focused on real results, not empty promises. Under his leadership, JAM Capital Consultants has become a trusted resource for clients who want practical solutions and long-term success.

"My story is proof that with the right guidance and determination, you can change your life and your credit for the better."
- Carnelius Johnson, Founder & CEO

Leadership Philosophy

Driven by a passion for empowering people, Carnelius has built JAM Capital Consultants to be more than just a credit consulting company. It is a place where clients feel heard, supported, and equipped with the knowledge to make confident financial decisions. Whether you are trying to buy a home, secure funding for your business, or simply clean up past mistakes, Carnelius and his team are committed to standing with you every step of the way.
