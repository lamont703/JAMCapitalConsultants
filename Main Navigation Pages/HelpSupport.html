Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my support request?
For email inquiries, we typically respond within 24 hours, though most customers receive a response much sooner. Text message support during business hours (Tuesday-Friday 10am-6pm EST, Saturday 11am-3pm EST) usually receives a response within 1-2 hours. For urgent matters, we recommend using text message support during business hours.
What kind of support is included in my subscription?
All paid plans include access to our email support (24/7) and text message support during business hours (Tuesday-Friday 10am-6pm EST, Saturday 11am-3pm EST). The level of personalized guidance varies by plan - DIY plans receive general guidance, while Guided and Full-Service plans include more in-depth, personalized support and strategy sessions.
Can I get help with understanding my credit report?
Absolutely! Our support team includes credit experts who can help you understand your credit report, identify potential errors, and develop strategies for improvement. For more in-depth analysis, consider our Guided or Full-Service plans which include personalized credit strategy sessions.
What if I need help outside of business hours?
Our email support is available 24/7, and we strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible, even outside business hours. Additionally, our knowledge base and AI-powered JAM Dispute Bot are available around the clock to provide immediate assistance with common questions and credit dispute strategies.
Do you offer support in languages other than English?
Yes, we currently offer support in English and Spanish. When contacting our support team, simply indicate your language preference, and we'll connect you with a team member who can assist you in your preferred language.
