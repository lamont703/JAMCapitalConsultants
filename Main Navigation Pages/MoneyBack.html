Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is covered by the money-back guarantee?
Our money-back guarantee covers the monthly service fees you've paid for our credit dispute services. If you meet all the eligibility requirements and don't see any improvements in your credit report after 6 months, we'll refund the service fees you've paid during that period. Setup fees and third-party costs are not included in the refund.
How do you define "improvement" in my credit report?
Improvement is defined as any positive change in your credit report, such as the removal of inaccurate negative items, corrections to errors, or an increase in your credit score. Even the removal of a single inaccurate item is considered an improvement, as it contributes to the overall health of your credit profile.
What if I've only been using the service for 3 months?
Our money-back guarantee requires a minimum commitment of 6 consecutive months. Credit dispute is a process that takes time, and meaningful results typically begin to appear after several rounds of disputes. If you cancel before the 6-month period, you won't be eligible for the guarantee.
How do I request a refund under the guarantee?
To request a refund, contact our customer service team at support@jamcapitalconsultants.com. You'll need to provide documentation showing your active participation in the program, including dispute records and before/after credit reports. Our team will review your account and respond to your request within 10 business days.
Can I get a partial refund if I've seen some improvement but not as much as expected?
Our guarantee is based on whether there has been any improvement, not the degree of improvement. If any positive changes have occurred in your credit report, regardless of how small, the guarantee conditions have been met. Credit dispute results vary based on individual circumstances, and we cannot guarantee specific outcomes or point increases.
