Late Payment Removal

Use this prompt to request removal of a one-time late payment due to hardship.

Late Payment Removal Prompt

[Your Full Name][Your Address][City State ZIP Code][Email Address][Phone Number]Date: [Insert Date]
                            To Whom It May Concern
                            I'm writing to request the removal of a late payment reported by [Creditor's Name] on [Insert Date] from my credit
                            report. I take full ownership of that missed payment, but I want to be transparent about the circumstances.
                            That moment was not a reflection of how I manage my responsibilities. I was going through a challenging time, and
                            unfortunately the payment slipped through the cracks. As soon as I recognized the mistake I got back on track and
                            brought the account current. Since then I've kept everything in good standing.
                            I'm not making excuses. I'm simply asking that you take into consideration my overall payment history and the effort
                            I've put into correcting the situation. I'm hoping you'll consider removing this late mark as a one-time courtesy or
                            goodwill adjustment. It would really help me continue rebuilding and moving forward financially.
                            Thank you for your time and for considering my request. I genuinely appreciate it.
                            Sincerely[Your Full Name]
